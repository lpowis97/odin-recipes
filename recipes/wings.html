Grilled Buffalo Wings

Description

The only thing better than buffalo wings are GRILLED buffalo wings! This recipe is super simple and amazing for your tailgate menu!

Ingredients

  • 12 whole chicken wings
  • 1 bottle of Frank's Red Hot

Steps

  1. Heat grill to 500 degrees
  2. Add wings to grill.
  3. Baste Frank's Red Hot onto each wing.
  4. Close grill lid and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Using grill tongs flip wings over.
  6. Baste Frank's Red Hot onto each wing.
  7. Grill for another 5 minutes or until wings are nice and charred.
  8. Enjoy!
