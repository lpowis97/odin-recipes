Beer Cheese Dip

Description

Beer Cheese Dip is a savory mixture of two of the best flavors ever: sharp cheese and IPA beer! It's up to you to decide what to dip in it; pita bread, tortilla chips, or my favorite, apples!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. shredded sharp cheddar
  • 8 oz. block of cream cheese
  • 1 12 oz. beer (preferably IPA!)

Steps

  1. Heat cast iron skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add beer and bring to a boil.
  3. Gradually add the 1 lb. of shredded cheese, pausing between handfuls to whisk until melted.
  4. Separate cream cheese block into chunks and add to skillet.
  5. Continually whisk until creamy and smooth.
  6. Take off heat and let cool for 10 min.
  7. Grab your favorite items to dip and enjoy!
