Potato & Onion Skillet

Description

The Potato & Onion Skillet is a tasty mix of seasoned breakfast potatoes and onions topped with over easy eggs.

Ingredients

  • 4 potatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 2 Tbspns of Olive Oil
  • 1/4 cup of Shredded Cheese
  • Tony's Creole Seasoning
  • 4 eggs
  • Cooking Spray

Steps

  1. Cube potatoes and transfer to a mixing bowl.
  2. Spray potatoes with cooking spray and coat them with Tony's.
  3. Cook potatoes in Airfryer at 400 degrees for 15 minutes.
  4. Chop onions and sautee in 2 Tbspns of Olive Oil until browned.
  5. Add potatoes to the onions and cover with shredded cheese.
  6. Transfer potatoes, onions, and cheese to two serving dishes.
  7. Fry eggs to over easy and two to the top of each dish.
  8. Enjoy!
